KNOW ABOUT DICE

  1. The word ‘dice’ is derived from Old French and Latin.
  2. Dice have been around for thousands of years.
  3. In olden days, people even played for much larger sums than they do today.
  4. All dice are slightly irregular although it’s visually invisible to the naked eye.
